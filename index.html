How it works

Create the record before the release goes live.

The page can exist while the details stay private. People can see that a disclosure is pending without seeing the PoC before the release window.

01

Upload the work

Add the PoC, technical write-up, affected product, company name, and timeline.

02

Keep the receipts

Track vendor outreach, replies, acknowledgements, notes, and status changes.

03

Release on purpose

When the timer ends, the disclosure enters a waiting period. The researcher can publish, cancel, or remove it.

Why this exists

Disclosure should not depend on fragile workarounds.

Researchers often end up using private repos, notes apps, email threads, screenshots, and scheduled blog posts. That works until it does not. OnlyVulns is meant to give the whole process a home.

Public pages

Show the disclosure status and timing before technical details are released.

Vendor logs

Keep the communication history attached to the disclosure record.

Researcher control

Cancel, remove, delay, or publish according to the release workflow.
